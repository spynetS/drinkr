SPACING & SHAPE CONVENTIONS
----------------------------
- Screen padding: 20–24px horizontal, 16–48px vertical
- Gap between elements: 8–16px (use gap property)
- Border radius scale: small=10, medium=14–16, large=20, card=24
- Always use SafeAreaView as the root container
- ScrollView grids use contentContainerStyle (not style) for flexDirection/alignItems/justifyContent

---

GENERAL RULES
-------------
1. Dark backgrounds only. Never use white or light backgrounds.
2. All surfaces use transparency and blur-like layering (glass-morphism).
3. Purple (#7c3aed) is the primary interactive color. Red is for danger/imposter. Green is for success/crewmate.
4. Use eyebrow labels (small uppercase tracked text) above every major section title.
5. Shadows on cards always use #7c3aed as shadowColor, not black.
6. Avoid solid gray. Use rgba whites with low opacity instead.
7. Buttons always have a matching glow shadow in their accent color.
8. Empty states use a large emoji, a bold label, and a muted subtitle.
9. Every screen has the two ambient glow blobs (purple top-left, red bottom-right).
10. Use StyleSheet.create() — avoid excessive inline styles.
